## What is a Build-to-Suit Office?

In a Build-to-Suit (BTS) agreement, the workspace provider (like WeeSpaces) leases a bare-shell commercial property and completely customizes the interior fit-out based on the specific requirements of the client. 

Unlike a standard coworking space where you rent pre-existing desks in a shared environment, a BTS office is a private, dedicated workspace designed exclusively for your team. 

## The Core Advantages of Build-to-Suit

### 1. 100% Brand Identity
A BTS office is *your* headquarters. From the moment employees or clients step off the elevator, they see your logo, your corporate colors, and your unique company culture reflected in the design. It doesn't look or feel like a shared workspace.

### 2. Custom Layouts and Workflows
Every company operates differently. 
* A software development firm might need dense workstation clusters and multiple soundproof scrum rooms.
* A BPO might require an open-plan layout with maximum desk density and robust acoustic paneling.
* A corporate headquarters might prioritize executive cabins, a grand boardroom, and a premium reception area.

With a Build-to-Suit model, you dictate the layout. 

### 3. Shift from CAPEX to OPEX
Fitting out a 10,000 sq ft [office space for 100 employees](/blog/office-space-for-100-employees) can cost upwards of ₹2 Crore in capital expenditure (CAPEX) for HVAC, networking, furniture, and partitions. 

In a BTS model, WeeSpaces absorbs that upfront capital cost. We amortize the cost of the fit-out into a predictable, monthly operational expenditure (OPEX). This keeps your capital free to invest in your actual business growth, not in real estate.

### 4. Enterprise-Grade IT and Security
You maintain total control over your IT infrastructure. A BTS office allows you to install dedicated server rooms, isolated VLANs, bespoke firewalls, and biometric access control systems tailored precisely to your global security policies.
