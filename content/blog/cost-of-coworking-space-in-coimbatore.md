## Traditional Office Rent vs. Coworking in Coimbatore

If you are considering renting an independent commercial space in Peelamedu or RS Puram, consider the hidden costs:

* **Heavy Deposits:** Traditional landlords often require 6-10 months of rent upfront. Coworking spaces require just 1-2 months.
* **Interior Fit-outs:** Building out a 1,000 sq.ft bare-shell office can easily cost ₹15 Lakhs+ for flooring, ACs, networking, and furniture.
* **Operational Overhead:** You must independently manage and pay for electricity, high-speed broadband, housekeeping, security, and pantry supplies.

When you factor in CapEx (Capital Expenditure) and OpEx (Operational Expenditure), a coworking or [managed office](/managed-office/coimbatore) is typically **20-30% more cost-effective** over a 2 to 3-year timeline.
