## 2. GST & Legal Compliance Made Easy

One of the largest hurdles to expanding into a new Indian state is taxation. If you want to warehouse goods, hire local employees, or invoice clients in Kerala or Tamil Nadu, you need a local GST registration.

However, the Goods and Services Tax (GST) department requires a valid, commercial physical address to grant registration. You cannot legally use a residential address for a Pvt. Ltd. company’s commercial GST registration.

**The Solution:**
A [Virtual Office for GST Registration](/legal/virtual-office-compliance) provides you with the exact documentation the government requires:
- A legally binding No Objection Certificate (NOC)
- A registered Rent Agreement
- Recent utility bills linking the property to the commercial landlord

With WeeSpaces, this documentation is processed and delivered at lightning speed, ensuring your new state branch is legally compliant immediately.
