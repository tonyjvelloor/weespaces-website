Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum) is the technological heart of Kerala. Anchored by the massive Technopark ecosystem, the city is a magnet for software development centers, deep-tech startups, and global IT enterprises. 

If you are planning to set up an office in Trivandrum, choosing the right location is critical for talent acquisition, client meetings, and employee satisfaction. Here are the best areas for IT startups to rent office space in Trivandrum.

## 1. Near Technopark (Kazhakkoottam)

Technopark is the undeniable epicenter of Trivandrum’s IT industry. Setting up near Kazhakkoottam puts you in the middle of a sprawling tech community. 

* **Pros:** Close proximity to major IT giants, excellent networking opportunities, and a high concentration of tech talent.
* **Cons:** Traffic congestion during peak hours and high demand for commercial real estate, which can drive up costs.

## 2. Pattom

Pattom strikes the perfect balance between city-center convenience and accessibility to the tech corridors. It’s rapidly becoming a favored destination for startups that want premium infrastructure without the rigid SEZ compliance rules of inside-the-park leases.

* **Pros:** Central location, fantastic connectivity to the airport and railway station, premium commercial buildings, and plenty of dining options for employees.
* **Cons:** Limited large-scale bare-shell commercial spaces compared to the outskirts (which is why coworking is popular here).

## 3. Vellayambalam & Sasthamangalam

These areas are traditionally known as the premium residential and commercial heart of Trivandrum. They offer a quieter, more upscale environment.

* **Pros:** Prestigious address, lush green surroundings, and close proximity to government offices and top-tier cafes.
* **Cons:** Further away from the core IT talent pool residing near Technopark.

## 4. Bypass Road (Chakkai to കഴക്കൂട്ടം)

The NH66 bypass has seen explosive commercial growth. It offers excellent connectivity and modern glass-facade buildings.

* **Pros:** Close to the airport, modern infrastructure, and proximity to Lulu Mall.
* **Cons:** Can feel slightly isolated from the traditional city center vibe.
