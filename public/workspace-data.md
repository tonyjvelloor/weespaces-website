## 4. Virtual Office Documentation
When signing up for a Virtual Office for GST or Company Registration, WeeSpaces provides:
1. Rent Agreement
2. NOC (No Objection Certificate) from the Legal Owner
3. Recent Utility Bill
4. Signage (Digital or Physical Name Board representation as required by law)
